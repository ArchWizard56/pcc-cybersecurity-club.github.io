#### How to add a page or chapter to this Wiki

If you want to add a section or an entire guide, you'll likely want to [Install Hugo](https://gohugo.io/getting-started/installing/), and a editor that helps with writing Markdown. I like [Visual Studio Code](https://code.visualstudio.com/).
