Welcome to the PCC² wiki

Here you’ll find a gathering of resources, guides, and other helpful information compiled by the Portland Community College Cybersecurity Club (PCC²). If you’re looking to get started, take a peek at the master resources page, migrated from our Discord, or look at some of our awesome guides. For more information about the club, see our homepage, and if you have anything that you think belongs here, see our guide on contributing to this wiki here.
